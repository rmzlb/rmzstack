# Content Performance Queries

Ready-to-use analysis prompts. Use these with your content log data
(see content-engine/references/content-log-template.md for the log format).

---

## Top Performers

### By Engagement
```
"Show me my top 5 posts by engagement rate this month."
"Which post got the most comments in the last 30 days?"
"List my top 3 posts by DMs received."
"What's my highest-performing post ever? What made it work?"
```

### By Conversion
```
"Which posts drove the most signups/sales?"
"What content led to DM conversations that converted?"
"Compare conversion rates: LinkedIn vs Email vs X."
"Which content pillar drives the most revenue?"
```

---

## Pattern Analysis

### Format Comparison
```
"Compare story posts vs framework posts vs contrarian posts on LinkedIn.
Show avg engagement rate, avg comments, and avg DMs for each."

"Which content format gets the most bookmarks on X?"

"Do threads outperform single tweets? By how much?"

"Compare my long posts (>1500 chars) vs short posts (<1000 chars).
Which gets higher engagement? Which drives more DMs?"
```

### Pillar Comparison
```
"Break down my content by pillar. Which pillar gets:
- Highest engagement rate?
- Most DMs?
- Most follower growth?
Show the last 3 months."

"Am I over-investing in a pillar that doesn't convert?
Show pillar-by-pillar: posts published vs conversions."
```

### Hook Analysis
```
"Categorize my last 20 LinkedIn hooks by style:
- Statement ('I almost quit.')
- Question ('What if onboarding doesn't matter?')
- Number ('47 features shipped. Revenue went down.')
- Contrarian ('The common advice is wrong.')
Then show avg engagement per style."

"Which of my hooks stopped the most scrolls? (highest impressions)"
"Which hooks converted best? (highest DMs per impression)"
```

### Timing Analysis
```
"Group my posts by day of week. Which day gets highest avg engagement?"
"Compare morning posts (before 10am) vs afternoon posts. Which performs better?"
"Is there a time slot where I consistently underperform? Should I stop posting then?"
```

---

## Trend Analysis

### Growth Tracking
```
"Plot my LinkedIn follower growth over the last 3 months.
When were the biggest spikes? What content caused them?"

"Is my engagement rate trending up or down over the last 8 weeks?
If down, when did it start declining?"

"Compare my avg impressions: month 1 vs month 2 vs month 3.
Am I reaching more people over time?"
```

### Content Velocity
```
"How many pieces did I publish per week over the last month?
Is there a correlation between publishing frequency and engagement?"

"Did my quality (avg roast score) go up or down as I published more?"
```

---

## Repurpose Analysis

```
"When I repurpose a LinkedIn post to X thread, does the thread
outperform a standalone X thread on the same topic?"

"Which repurposed format consistently outperforms the original?
(e.g., does the carousel version of a post get more reach?)"

"What's my best repurpose workflow by ROI?
(most engagement per minute spent adapting)"
```

---

## Competitive Comparison

```
"Based on my content log, where do I sit vs benchmarks?
- LinkedIn: am I above or below 5% engagement?
- Email: am I above or below 35% open rate?
- X: am I above or below 3% engagement?"

"Which metrics am I strong on? Which need work?"
```

---

## Decision Prompts

These prompts help you decide what to do next:

```
"Based on my last month's data, what ONE change would have
the biggest impact on my results?"

"I have time to write 3 posts this week. Based on past performance,
what format + pillar combination should I use?"

"Should I keep investing time in X/Twitter? Compare my X results
vs LinkedIn results per hour invested."

"What should I test next based on my current data gaps?"
```

---

## How to Use These

1. Keep a content log (use content-engine's log template)
2. After 10+ entries, start running these queries
3. Review weekly (pick 2-3 queries, not all of them)
4. Act on the answers (not just interesting, actionable)
5. Feed insights back into content-engine briefs and marketing-strategy reviews
