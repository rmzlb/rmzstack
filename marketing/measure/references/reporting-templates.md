# Reporting Templates

Ready-to-use templates for weekly, monthly, and quarterly marketing reports.

---

## Weekly Content Report (5 minutes, every Monday)

### Template

```markdown
## Week of YYYY-MM-DD

### Published
| # | Platform | Format | Topic | Engagement Rate |
|---|----------|--------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | LinkedIn | Story | [topic] | X.X% |
| 2 | X/Twitter | Thread | [topic] | X.X% |
| 3 | Email | Nurture | [topic] | X.X% open / X.X% click |

### Best Performer
- **Post**: [which one]
- **Why it worked**: [1 sentence — hook? topic? timing?]
- **Engagement**: [key number]

### Worst Performer
- **Post**: [which one]
- **Why it flopped**: [1 sentence — weak hook? wrong audience? bad timing?]
- **Lesson**: [what to change]

### Pattern Spotted
- [e.g., "Contrarian posts get 2x comments vs story posts"]

### Next Week Plan
- [ ] [Content piece 1 — based on this week's data]
- [ ] [Content piece 2]
- [ ] [Test: specific thing to A/B test]
```

### Filled Example

```markdown
## Week of 2024-03-18

### Published
| # | Platform | Format | Topic | Engagement Rate |
|---|----------|--------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | LinkedIn | Contrarian | "Onboarding is overrated" | 6.2% |
| 2 | X/Twitter | Thread | Same topic repurposed | 3.1% |
| 3 | Email | Nurture #4 | Retention metrics | 38% open / 4.7% click |
| 4 | LinkedIn | Framework | "3 metrics that actually matter" | 3.8% |

### Best Performer
- **Post**: "Onboarding is overrated" (LinkedIn)
- **Why it worked**: Ultra-short contrarian hook stopped scroll. Got 38 comments because people disagreed (debate = engagement).
- **Engagement**: 14,200 impressions, 6.2% engagement, 7 DMs

### Worst Performer
- **Post**: "3 metrics that actually matter" (LinkedIn)
- **Why it flopped**: Hook was generic. "3 metrics" sounds like every other framework post. No tension.
- **Lesson**: Framework posts need a contrarian angle in the hook, not just "here are N things"

### Pattern Spotted
- Contrarian hooks consistently outperform framework hooks (3rd week in a row)
- Tuesday 8am posts get 40% more impressions than Thursday posts

### Next Week Plan
- [ ] LinkedIn: Contrarian post on hiring (test if pattern holds outside product topics)
- [ ] X Thread: Repurpose onboarding post (ride the momentum)
- [ ] Email: Case study on retention metrics (deepen the nurture sequence)
- [ ] Test: Same topic, contrarian hook vs question hook — compare engagement
```

## Quarterly Strategy Review (2 hours, every 3 months)

### Template

```markdown
## Q[N] YYYY Strategy Review

### Business Impact
- Revenue attributed to marketing: [$]
- Pipeline generated: [$]
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): [$]
- Marketing ROI: [X]x

### Channel Performance
| Channel | Spend | Leads | Customers | CAC | ROI |
|---------|-------|-------|-----------|-----|-----|
| Organic LinkedIn | $0 (time) | [n] | [n] | [$] | [x] |
| Organic X | $0 (time) | [n] | [n] | [$] | [x] |
| Email | [$tools] | [n] | [n] | [$] | [x] |
| Paid ads | [$spend] | [n] | [n] | [$] | [x] |
| SEO | [$tools] | [n] | [n] | [$] | [x] |

### Strategy Validation
- [ ] ICP still accurate? (compare new customers to ICP definition)
- [ ] Positioning still differentiated? (check competitor moves)
- [ ] Messaging resonating? (compare top posts' language to messaging matrix)
- [ ] Right channels? (any channel <1x ROI = consider cutting)

### Key Learnings
1. [Biggest insight from this quarter]
2. [Second biggest insight]
3. [What surprised you]

### Next Quarter Plan
- **Strategy changes**: [any ICP/positioning/messaging updates → update marketing-strategy]
- **Channel allocation**: [shift budget/time based on ROI data]
- **Content focus**: [which pillars/formats to prioritize]
- **Tests planned**: [2-3 experiments for next quarter]
```

## Decision Framework

After every report, answer ONE question:

**"Based on this data, what's the ONE thing we should change?"**

Not 5 things. ONE. The highest-impact change based on the clearest signal.

Examples:
- "Our contrarian posts get 3x engagement. Write 2 per week instead of 1."
- "Email click rate dropped 40%. Our CTAs are too vague. Test specific CTAs."
- "LinkedIn drives 80% of our DMs. Stop investing time in X until LinkedIn plateaus."
- "Tuesday mornings outperform every other time. Move all posts to Tuesday 8am."
